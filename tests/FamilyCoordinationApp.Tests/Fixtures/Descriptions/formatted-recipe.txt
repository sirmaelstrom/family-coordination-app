Classic Chicken Stir-Fry

Follow me for more recipes! New video every Tuesday.

Ingredients:
- 1 lb chicken breast, sliced thin
- 2 cups broccoli florets
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- 1/2 cup chicken broth

Instructions:
1. Heat oil in a large wok over high heat.
2. Add chicken and cook until golden, about 5-7 minutes.
3. Add garlic and broccoli, stir-fry for 3 minutes.
4. Pour in soy sauce and chicken broth, toss to combine.
5. Drizzle with sesame oil and serve immediately.

Prep: 15 min
Cook time: 20 min
Serves 4
