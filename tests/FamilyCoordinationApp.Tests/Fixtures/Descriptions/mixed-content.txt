Hey friends! Today I'm sharing my grandmother's tomato soup recipe. She used to make
this every Sunday and the whole house would smell amazing. I've been working on
recreating it for years and I finally got it right.

A few notes before we begin: use the ripest tomatoes you can find, and please don't
skip the step of roasting them first. It makes a huge difference.

Ingredients:
- 3 lbs ripe tomatoes, halved
- 1 large yellow onion, quartered
- 4 cloves garlic
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 2 cups vegetable broth
- 1/2 cup heavy cream
- Fresh basil, for garnish

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
2. Toss tomatoes, onion, and garlic with olive oil, salt, and pepper.
3. Roast for 35 minutes until caramelized.
4. Transfer to blender with broth and blend until smooth.
5. Stir in heavy cream and reheat gently.
6. Serve with fresh basil.
